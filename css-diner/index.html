TOY STORY

Toy Story was the first feature film from Pixar Studios. There are now four Toy Story films in total, but without the fourth, the trilogy is perfect. This movie introduced us to iconic quotes like "To Infinity And Beyond!" and characters like Buzz and Woody.

CARS

Life is a highway... on Route 66 in this trilogy of movies. Lightning McQueen and Tow Mater are now well known characters as a result of these films. While the second movie is terrible, the trilogy creates an amazing story that makes you actually cry over a car.

MONSTERS INC

Mike and Sully are the least scary monsters you'll ever see, but that's why you fall in love with them. The prequel movie should not be spoken of, but it exists. As we follow along with Boo and the monsters, you are entranced by the strange but unique plot.

UP

I will take no criticism. THIS IS THE BEST PIXAR FILM. From the sense of adventure to the TALKING DOGS, this movie cannot be beat. Within the first fifteen minutes, you are sobbing over Carl and Ellie. By the end, you cannot help but yell "Adventure is out there!" There is no better movie than Up. Period.

THE GOOD DINOSAUR

I do not care what anyone says this is a good movie and it made me cry. It is probably the most underappreciated Pixar movie, but Arlo and Spot are adorable. My favorite part, however, is the animation. Each scene and background is just stunning and makes the movie completely worth watching.

COCO

Un Poco Loco... This movie is beautiful and heartbreaking. It was the first Pixar movie that represented the Hispanic heritage and many loved it for that reason. The music is incredible and it is such a fun movie.
